# Dolce Mondo Business Overview

Dolce Mondo is a premium global lifestyle brand focused on elegant cultural experiences through handcrafted sweets, authentic global coffee, globally inspired energy refreshers, delivered via a low-capital Micro Boutique format embedded inside high-traffic grocery retailers rather than standalone real estate.

We identify a cultural tradition, craft it into an approachable premium product, and distribute it through the location and traffic our retail partners already have.

## Distribution Channels

1. **Micro Boutiques** (H-E-B and similar retail partners) — primary discovery channel.
2. **E-commerce** — full product line, experience kits, and subscriptions, available nationwide (Square platform).
3. **Wholesale & Hospitality** — cafés, hotels, and offices that carry our products.
4. **Flagship location** — future longer-term destination once the model is proven at scale (not near-term priority).

## Proof: Traction

- $70,000+ in revenue in under 6 months from a single Micro Boutique location (before city permitting shutdown paused operations).
- 45%+ repeat customer rate — customers returned; not a one-time novelty.
- Vietnamese Sweet Coffee — top-selling product, validated as the hero experience and entry point for new customers.
- First location operated inside H-E-B, one of Texas's largest grocery retailers. Permitting resolution underway; reopening expected in the coming weeks.
